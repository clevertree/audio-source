Audio Source Composer

This is an early peek at the Audio Source Composer. Hit 'play' to hear it in action. Follow our Facebook for release updates. Visit our Discord and tell us what you think!

Currently Working:

  • Add, edit, and delete notes and note groups
  • Recursively play back note groups
  • Note velocity, sample key mapping
  • Sample Synthesizer Instrument with GM library
  • Tracker-View with delta rows
  • Works on any modern browser and hardware
  • Embeddable customElement for editor, and synthesizer
  • Import MIDI files (buggy)

Planned Features:

  • Embeddable customElement for player
  • Oscillator Synthesizer (No libraries yet)
  • Audio editing
  • ADSR
  • XY Grid for easy mouse-click composition
  • Real-time editing between multiple users (like Google Docs)
  • Server-side song rendering
  • Export MIDI files

How to embed the composer element on your website

<script src="https://audiosource.io/composer/audio-source-composer.min.js"></script>
<audio-source-composer></audio-source-composer>
